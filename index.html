About me

My name is Hannes Kneiding. I'm a university graduate with a Master's degree in Computational Sciences obtained at Freie Universität Berlin, and PhD student in the CompSci doctoral program at the University of Oslo. I am interested in computational materials science based on quantum-mechanical calculations. My main focus so far lies on the employment of machine learning methods in conjunction with approaches from quantum chemistry for optimising specific properties in candidate molecules of predefined molecular classes.
